{\itshape The Nomad} is a 2D action RPG set in a post-\/apocalypse desert hellscape taking place in the final sane dream of a God of War going insane.\hypertarget{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md478}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{History}{History}}\label{md__r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md478}
The Nomad was a game I originally created in my own modified version of the Quake III Arena engine. But now it\textquotesingle{}s in the Godot Engine to speed up and streamline the development process.
